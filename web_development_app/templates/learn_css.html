Network automation is the process of automating the configuration, management, operation & testing of physical and virtual devices within a network. With growing costs, complexity of networks and the daily emergence of bandwidth-hungry applications, networks cannot be managed manually. Multiple touch points, remote operations result in delays affecting critical business initiatives.
